Faces of Elmina

The Emina Slave Castle was the first European trading post in Sub-Saharan Africa. It became one of the main points of departure in the Transatlantic Slave Trade. The book explores the residents of Elmina's residual relationship with the legacy of the Elmina Slave Castle through a series interviews and photographs.
