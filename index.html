Faces of Elmina

An photojournalistic examination of the cultural residue of slavery

The Emina Slave Castle was the first European trading post in Sub-Saharan Africa. It became one of the main points of departure in the Transatlantic Slave Trade. This book explores the residents of Elmina's residual relationship with the legacy of the Elmina Slave Castle through a series of interviews and photographs.

Flowmoji

A private messaging application with added mass-surveilance resistance

This project builds mass-surveillance resistance into the Signal iOS application by sending bogus messages between chatters in the background. As a result, MITM attackers quickly exhaust their resources since only a small portion of intercepted messages that are decrypted using brute-force or exploiting as-of-yet unknown weaknesses in the Signal protocol will contain real, usable content.

Sniffer

Exposing the unseen privacy infringements of the wireless world

This project is designed to expose the personal information individuals' devices are constantly publishing without their knowledge or consent. Sniffer continously queries the Wi-Fi networks for publically broadcasted mDNS-SD packets with information including the device's name. Sniffer parses these packets using regex for human names and speaks these names aloud.
